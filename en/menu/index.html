Cuvée Terlaner, Alto Adige DOC

  • Region: Alto Adige
  • Winery: Cantina Terlano
  • Grapes: Chardonnay, Sauvignon Blanc, Pinot bianco (Weißburgunder)
  • Bottle size: 750mL
  • Alcohol content: 13.5%

Our historic cuvée, which has been produced since the winery was founded, is a very complex wine thanks to the composition of the three most traditional Terlan white wine varieties: Pinot Blanc, Chardonnay and Sauvignon Blanc. Pinot Blanc, which makes up the majority, provides freshness and a good acidity structure, Chardonnay flatters with softness and warmth, while Sauvignon complements the cuvée with its fine aromas. On the nose, green apple and white peach characterize the classic Terlan, which also has a fine nuance of lemon balm and mint. The peach aroma is also very strong on the palate and brings the Terlan to life. The multifaceted, structured taste is characterized by an interplay of freshness and minerality, but at the same time also has a great length.

42 €

Il Bruciato, Antinori

  • Region: Bolgheri, Toscana
  • Winery: Antinori
  • Grapes: Cabernet Sauvignon, Merlot, Syrah
  • Bottle size: 750mL
  • Alcohol content: 14%

Bruciato was born in 2002, from one of the most difficult harvests of Tenuta Guado al Tasso, to tell and make known the unique terroir of Bolgheri in a modern style. The carefully selected grapes of Cabernet Sauvignon, Merlot and Syrah come from vineyards with very different geological soils, which give great complexity. The Bruciato presents itself in the usual intense ruby red color. On the nose, notes of ripe red berries predominate, accompanied by pleasant hints of sweet spices and light nuances of mint and tobacco aromas. On the palate, the wine is well structured, full-bodied and with a beautiful fruity aftertaste that gives it pleasant drinkability and persistence.

  • Region: Toscana
  • Winery: Villa Poggio Salvi
  • Grapes: Sangiovese
  • Bottle size: 750mL
  • Alcohol content: 14.5%

The special location of the Villa Poggio Salvi estate enables the production of an exceptional, very elegant Brunello, from Sangiovese grapes picked by hand with great care. A short path lies between the vineyards and the wine-making cellar, hindering an undesired fermentation start or eventual transport damages to the grape-stones. Vino spumante

Sparkling wine

Blanc de Blancs - Franciacorta, Marchese Antinori

  • Region: Toscana
  • Winery: Marchese Antinori
  • Grapes: Chardonnay
  • Bottle size: 750mL
  • Alcohol content: 12%

This Blanc de Blancs is made from the finest Chardonnay grapes, sometimes with a small portion of Pinot Bianco, creating an elegant blend of refined freshness. The long aging on the lees lends the wine a vibrant floral and fruity profile, great balance, and finesse. Pale yellow in color, the wine has a creamy foam with a fine, persistent perlage. The nose is fragrant and immediate, with floral and fruity notes reminiscent of apple and white-fleshed peach. The palate is strikingly lively and fresh, accompanied by good balance and distinct elegance.
