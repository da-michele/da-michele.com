Sauvignon Vendemmia, La Tunella

  • Region: Friuli orientale
  • Winery: La Tunella
  • Grapes: Sauvignon Blanc
  • Bottle size: 750mL
  • Alcohol content: 13%

The wine is characterized by a very intense and persistent scent and a typical variety in the nose. Intense and fine scents predominate with typical herbal aromas such as sage and tomato leaf, which mix with fruity and delicate hints of honeydew melon and yellow pepper. The taste is convincing with its perfectly balanced ratio between freshness and spicy fullness. Intense and palate-enveloping bouquet, the finish is aromatic and persistent.

Terebinto, Planeta

  • Region: Menfi, Sicilia
  • Winery: Planeta
  • Grapes: Grillo
  • Bottle size: 750mL
  • Alcohol content: 14%

Canary yellow in color. On the nose, it is a tropical version of this extraordinary, never predictable Sicilian grape variety. Aromas of pineapple, white melon, rice, Granny Smith apple, prickly pear. Fresh and vibrant in the mouth and very well balanced thanks to the altitude of the vineyards.

39 €

Vino rosato

Rosé wine

La Mora Rosato, Cecchi

  • Region: Maremma, Toscana
  • Winery: Cecchi
  • Grapes: Sangiovese
  • Bottle size: 750mL
  • Alcohol content: 12.5%

With its favorable climatic characteristics, together with the natural vocation of the soils, Maremma guarantees the ideal setting for obtaining elegant and extremely pleasant wines. La Mora Rosato has a bright antique pink color. The nose is rich, with notes of fresh fruit and aromatic herbs. Well integrated acidity that guarantees a good persistence.

Vino rosso

Red wine

Botrosecco, Le Mortelle

  • Region: Maremma, Toscana
  • Winery: Le Mortelle
  • Grapes: Cabernet Sauvignon, Cabernet Franc
  • Bottle size: 750mL
  • Alcohol content: 14.5%

The color is characterized by a dense ruby-red with a violet edge. The striking bouquet shows fully ripe cherries, gentle berries, creamy oak, fine chocolate and a hint of balsamic notes. On the palate, it has that multifaceted, seductive richness that only a Supertuscan of this cut can bring. Power combines with finesse, fruit with elegance, barrel aromas with harmonious tartaric acid, everything remains perfectly balanced and garnished with ripe tannins. The classy aftertaste is also worthy of a Supertuscan.

54 €

Brunello di Montalcino, Villa Poggio Salvi

  • Region: Toscana
  • Winery: Villa Poggio Salvi
  • Grapes: Sangiovese
  • Bottle size: 750mL
  • Alcohol content: 14.5%

The special location of the Villa Poggio Salvi estate enables the production of an exceptional, very elegant Brunello, from Sangiovese grapes picked by hand with great care. A short path lies between the vineyards and the wine-making cellar, hindering an undesired fermentation start or eventual transport damages to the grape-stones. Vino spumante

Sparkling wine

Blanc de Blancs - Franciacorta, Marchese Antinori

  • Region: Toscana
  • Winery: Marchese Antinori
  • Grapes: Chardonnay
  • Bottle size: 750mL
  • Alcohol content: 12%

This Blanc de Blancs is made from the finest Chardonnay grapes, sometimes with a small portion of Pinot Bianco, creating an elegant blend of refined freshness. The long aging on the lees lends the wine a vibrant floral and fruity profile, great balance, and finesse. Pale yellow in color, the wine has a creamy foam with a fine, persistent perlage. The nose is fragrant and immediate, with floral and fruity notes reminiscent of apple and white-fleshed peach. The palate is strikingly lively and fresh, accompanied by good balance and distinct elegance.

75 €
